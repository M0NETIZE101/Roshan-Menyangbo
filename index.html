About Roshan

Born in the
vertical world.

Roshan Menyanbo is not just a climber — he is a force of nature in human form. His raw physical dominance and mental fortitude are so extraordinary that competitors don't just lose to him, they find themselves outclimbed before they've even gripped the first hold.

With certifications from the International Federation of Sport Climbing and guided expeditions across six continents, Roshan has carved a reputation built not on recklessness — but on precision, patience, and an almost spiritual relationship with rock and ice.

Philosophy

The Vertical
Mindset.

"The mountain does not know your name, your fears, or your ambitions.
It only knows if you showed up."

Precision

Every route is studied for weeks. Every anchor point triple-checked. Excellence is not an accident — it is the result of relentless preparation.

Patience

The mountain reveals itself on its own terms. Waiting for the right moment — this is not weakness, it is wisdom carved from altitude.

Presence

At altitude, the mind cannot wander. Each handhold is a conversation with the present moment. Climbing is meditation at the edge of the possible.
